Our Activity

Electrical Consultancy
Electrical consultancy services encompass a range of expert advice and support

Overall, electrical consultancy aims to provide comprehensive expertise to help clients make informed decisions and achieve their electrical objectives efficiently and effectively.

H.T.& L.T. Installation
H.T. installations typically involve electrical systems with higher voltages

Conversely, L.T. installations typically deal with lower voltage systems found in residential, small commercial, and some industrial settings.

Electrical Estimation & Design
Electrical estimation and design services involve the planning, estimation, and design

These services are essential in ensuring that electrical systems are designed efficiently, cost-effectively, and in compliance with relevant regulations and standards.

Industrial/Commercial Electrification
Process of designing, implementing, and maintaining electrical systems

Industrial and commercial electrification refers to the process of designing,maintaining electrical systems tailored for the needs of industrial establishments.

Service/Prod Industrial Maintenance
It refers to the comprehensive upkeep and support of machinery

This type of maintenance encompasses various tasks aimed at preventing equipment failure, minimizing downtime, and extending the lifespan of assets.

Manufacturing of Control Panels
Involves the assembly and fabrication of specialized enclosures that house electrical.

These panels are crucial for regulating the operation of complex systems, ensuring safety, efficiency, and reliability in manufacturing environments.

Liaisoning Work
Involves facilitating communication and cooperation between different parties

The primary objective is to build and maintain positive relationships, resolve conflicts, and ensure the smooth flow of information and collaboration between stakeholders.

D. G. Set Registration
Process of obtaining official approval or authorization from relevant regulatory authorities

This process is typically required to ensure compliance with local regulations, environmental standards, and safety requirements.

Electrical Installation
Process of setting up electrical systems and components

It encompasses the planning, design, installation, testing, and maintenance of electrical wiring, devices, equipment, and fixtures to ensure safe and efficient electrical distribution.
